Thick Pizza Crust

Ingredients§

Active dry yeast1 package
Sugar1 teaspoons
Water at 110 °F1 cup
Water at 110 °F1/2 cup
Olive oil3 tablepoons
Bread flour3 1/2 cups
Salt2 teaspoons

Baking§

450 °F20 minutes

Directions§

  1. On a bowl, activate the yeast by mixing:

    Active dry yeast1 package
    Sugar1 teaspoons
    Water at 110 °F1 cup
  2. Wait for 5–10 minutes until foamy.

  3. Mix in the wet ingredients:

    Water at 110 °F1/2 cup
    Olive oil3 tablepoons

  4. Mix in the dry ingredients:

    Bread flour3 1/2 cups
    Salt2 teaspoons
  5. Cover the bowl.
  6. Let the dough rise on a warm place for 1 hour.
  7. Split the dough in two equal parts.
  8. Shape each part into a ball.
  9. On a pizza pan, roll each ball into a thin crust.
  10. With a fork, pinch holes all over the crusts.
  11. Coat the crusts in olive oil.
  12. Add tomato sauce and toppings.

  13. Bake:

    450 °F20 minutes

Serving§

2 pizza crusts.
