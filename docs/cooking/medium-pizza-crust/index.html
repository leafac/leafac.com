Medium Pizza Crust

Ingredients§

Unbleached all-purpose flour3 cups
Instant yeast1/2 teaspoon
Salt1 3/4 teaspoons
Seeds (optional)To taste
Water at 110 °FFrom 1 1/4 to 1 1/2 cups

Baking§

450 °F20 minutes

Directions§

  1. On a bowl, mix the dry ingredients:

    Unbleached all-purpose flour3 cups
    Instant yeast1/2 teaspoon
    Salt1 3/4 teaspoons
    Seeds (optional)To taste
  2. The amount of water depends on the weather. The result must be wet-to-the-hand.

    Mix in the wet ingredients:

    Water at 110 °FFrom 1 1/4 to 1 1/2 cups
  3. Cover the bowl.
  4. Let the dough rise on a warm place for 12–18 hours, until it doubles in size.
  5. Split the dough in two equal parts.
  6. Shape each part into a ball.
  7. On a pizza pan, roll each ball into a thin crust.
  8. With a fork, pinch holes all over the crusts.
  9. Coat the crusts in olive oil.
  10. Add tomato sauce and toppings.

  11. Bake:

    450 °F20 minutes

Serving§

2 pizza crusts.
