Thin Pizza Crust

Ingredients§

Unbleached all-purpose flour1 3/4 cups
Semolina flour1 1/4 cups
Instant yeast1 teaspoon
Salt1 teaspoon
Water at 110 °FFrom 1 to 1 1/4 cups
Olive oil1/4 cup

Baking§

450 °F15 minutes

Directions§

  1. On a bowl, mix the dry ingredients:

    Unbleached all-purpose flour1 3/4 cups
    Semolina flour1 1/4 cups
    Instant yeast1 teaspoon
    Salt1 teaspoon

  2. On a measuring cup, mix the wet ingredients:

    Water at 110 °FFrom 1 to 1 1/4 cups
    Olive oil1/4 cup
  3. Mix the dry and the wet ingredients together.
  4. Coat the dough and the bowl in olive oil.
  5. Cover the bowl.
  6. Let the dough rise on a warm place for 2–4 hours.
  7. Split the dough in two equal parts.
  8. Shape each part into a ball.
  9. On a pizza pan, roll each ball into a thin crust.
  10. With a fork, pinch holes all over the crusts.
  11. Coat the crusts in olive oil.
  12. Add tomato sauce and toppings.

  13. Bake:

    450 °F15 minutes

Serving§

2 pizza crusts.
