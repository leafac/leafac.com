Strawberry Chocolate Mousse Pie

Ingredients§

Dried mulberry1 1/2 cups
Pecan1/2 cup
Date1 cup
Cinnamon1 teaspoon
Banana3
Avocado1
Date1/2 cup
Cocoa powder1/4 cup
Maple syrup2 tablespoons
Cinnamon1 tablespoon
Water1/4 cup
Strawberry3
Shredded coconut1/4 cup

Directions§

  1. On a food processor, process the ingredients for the crust:

    Dried mulberry1 1/2 cups
    Pecan1/2 cup
    Date1 cup
    Cinnamon1 teaspoon
  2. Press crust into a pie pan.

  3. On a food processor, process the ingredients for the mousse:

    Banana3
    Avocado1
    Date1/2 cup
    Cocoa powder1/4 cup
    Maple syrup2 tablespoons
    Cinnamon1 tablespoon
    Water1/4 cup
  4. Pour mousse into crust.

  5. Add toppings:

    Strawberry3
    Shredded coconut1/4 cup

Serving§

1 strawberry chocolate mousse pie.
