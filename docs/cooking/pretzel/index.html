Pretzel

Ingredients§

Unbleached all-purpose flour2 1/2 cups
Salt1 1/2 teaspoons
Granulated sugar1 1/2 teaspoons
Instant yeast2 1/4 teaspoons
WaterFrom 3/4 to 1 cup
Water1 cup
Baking soda2 tablespoons
Coarse saltTo taste

Baking§

475 °F12 minutes

Directions§

  1. On a bowl, mix dry ingredients:

    Unbleached all-purpose flour2 1/2 cups
    Salt1 1/2 teaspoons
    Granulated sugar1 1/2 teaspoons
    Instant yeast2 1/4 teaspoons

  2. Mix in the wet ingredients:

    WaterFrom 3/4 to 1 cup
  3. Cover bowl.
  4. Let it rest for 45 minutes.
  5. Devide the dough in 10 equal parts.
  6. Shape each part into a ball, and then into pretzel form.

  7. On a pan, boil water and add the baking soda:

    Water1 cup
    Baking soda2 tablespoons
  8. Dip pretzels into boiling water for 20 seconds.

  9. Add salt on top:

    Coarse saltTo taste

  10. Bake:

    475 °F12 minutes

Serving§

10 pretzels.
