Chocolate Chip Cookie

Ingredients§

Coconut oil1/2 cup
Cocoa powder2 tablespoons
Granulated sugar1/4 cup
Light brown sugar1/3 cup
Almond milk1/4 cup
Golden flaxseed meal1 tablespoon
Vanilla extract2 teaspoons
Salt1/8 teaspoon
Baking soda1/2 teaspoon
Bleached all-purpose flour1 1/2 cup
Chocolate chips1/2 cup

Baking§

350 °F12 minutes

Directions§

  1. On a bowl, mix base ingredients one by one:

    Coconut oil1/2 cup
    Cocoa powder2 tablespoons
    Granulated sugar1/4 cup
    Light brown sugar1/3 cup
    Almond milk1/4 cup
    Golden flaxseed meal1 tablespoon
    Vanilla extract2 teaspoons
    Salt1/8 teaspoon
    Baking soda1/2 teaspoon

  2. Mix in the flour and cholocate chips in two steps:

    Bleached all-purpose flour1 1/2 cup
    Chocolate chips1/2 cup
  3. Shape the dough into cookies on a parchment paper. The cookie size—and, thus, the quantity—varies to taste.

  4. Bake:

    350 °F12 minutes
  5. Let it rest on a cooling rack.

Serving§

Around 12 cookies, depending on the size.
