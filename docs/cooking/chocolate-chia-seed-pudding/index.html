Chocolate Chia Seed Pudding

Ingredients§

Chia seeds1/3 cup
Water1/3 cup
Almond milk1 1/2 cup
Cocoa powder1/4 cup
Dates9
Cinnamon1/2 teaspoon
Salt1/4 teaspoon
Vanilla extract1/2 teaspoon

Directions§

  1. In a measuring cup, mix the chia seeds with water for 5 minutes:

    Chia seeds1/3 cup
    Water1/3 cup

  2. In a blender, mix the resulting paste with the following ingredients:

    Almond milk1 1/2 cup
    Cocoa powder1/4 cup

  3. Mix the dates in:

    Dates9

  4. Mix the rest of the ingredients in:

    Cinnamon1/2 teaspoon
    Salt1/4 teaspoon
    Vanilla extract1/2 teaspoon
  5. Let it rest in the fridge.

Serving§

2 cups.
