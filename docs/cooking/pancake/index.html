Pancake

This recipe is for sweet pancakes. For savory pancakes, add a bit more salt and less sugar.

Ingredients§

Bleached all-purpose flour1 cup
Light brown sugar1 tablespoon
Baking powder2 teaspoons
Salt1/8 teaspoon
Walnuts (optional)To taste
Almond milk1 cup
Vegetable oil2 tablespoons

Directions§

  1. On a bowl, mix dry ingredients:

    Bleached all-purpose flour1 cup
    Light brown sugar1 tablespoon
    Baking powder2 teaspoons
    Salt1/8 teaspoon
    Walnuts (optional)To taste

  2. Mix in the wet ingredients:

    Almond milk1 cup
    Vegetable oil2 tablespoons
  3. Fry.

Serving§

6 pancakes.
