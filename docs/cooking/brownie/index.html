Brownie

Ingredients§

Golden flaxseed meal2 tablespoons
Water5 tablespoons
Coconut oil1/2 cup
Granulated sugar3/4 cup
Vanilla extract2 teaspoons
Baking powder3/4 teaspoon
Salt1/8 teaspoon
Cocoa powder1/2 cup
Bleached all-purpose flour3/4 cup
Chocolate chips1/2 cup

Baking§

350 °F25 minutes

Directions§

  1. On a bowl, mix ingredients for flax egg:

    Golden flaxseed meal2 tablespoons
    Water5 tablespoons
  2. Wait for 5 minutes.

  3. Mix in the ingredients for the base one by one:

    Coconut oil1/2 cup
    Granulated sugar3/4 cup
    Vanilla extract2 teaspoons
    Baking powder3/4 teaspoon
    Salt1/8 teaspoon
    Cocoa powder1/2 cup

  4. Mix in the flour and cholocate chips in two steps:

    Bleached all-purpose flour3/4 cup
    Chocolate chips1/2 cup
  5. Shape the dough into brownies on a muffin pan.

  6. Bake:

    350 °F25 minutes
  7. Let it rest on a cooling rack.

Serving§

8 brownies.
