Banoffee Pie

Ingredients§

Dates1 cup
Water1/2 cup
Water2 tablespoons
Coconut oil4 tablespoons
Dates4
Almond meal1 cup
Oats1 1/2 cups
Vanilla extract1 teaspoon
Maple syrup3 tablespoons
Almond or peanut butter3 tablespoons
Salt1/8 teaspoon
Refrigerated coconut cream1 can
Powdered sugar2 teaspoons
Vanilla extract1 teaspoon
Banana1–2
Chocolate sauce1 tablespoon

Baking§

355 °F15 minutes

Directions§

  1. On a bowl, add water to the dates:

    Dates1 cup
    Water1/2 cup
  2. Let it rest for 5 minutes.

  3. Meanwhile, in a food processor, process the ingredients for base:

    Water2 tablespoons
    Coconut oil4 tablespoons
    Dates4
    Almond meal1 cup
    Oats1 1/2 cups
  4. Spread thin the resulting batter on a pie pan.

  5. Bake batter:

    355 °F15 minutes
  6. Process the soaked dates from before.

  7. Mix into the food processor the rest of the ingredients for the caramel:

    Vanilla extract1 teaspoon
    Maple syrup3 tablespoons
    Almond or peanut butter3 tablespoons
    Salt1/8 teaspoon

  8. With a hand mixer, mix ingredients for the cream:

    Refrigerated coconut cream1 can
    Powdered sugar2 teaspoons
    Vanilla extract1 teaspoon

  9. Add the caramel, toppings and cream to the crust that came out of the oven:

    Banana1–2
    Chocolate sauce1 tablespoon

Serving§

1 pie.
