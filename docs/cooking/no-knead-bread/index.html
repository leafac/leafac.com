No-Knead Bread

Ingredients§

Unbleached all-purpose flour3 cups
Instant yeast1/2 teaspoon
Salt1 3/4 teaspoons
Seeds or sun-dried tomatoes (optional)To taste
Water at 110 °FFrom 1 1/4 to 1 1/2 cups

Baking§

450 °F30 minutesEmpty covered pot
30 minutesBread in covered pot
15 minutesBread in uncovered pot

Directions§

  1. On a bowl, mix the dry ingredients:

    Unbleached all-purpose flour3 cups
    Instant yeast1/2 teaspoon
    Salt1 3/4 teaspoons
    Seeds or sun-dried tomatoes (optional)To taste
  2. The amount of water depends on the weather. The result must be wet-to-the-hand.

    Mix in the wet ingredients:

    Water at 110 °FFrom 1 1/4 to 1 1/2 cups
  3. Cover the bowl.
  4. Let the dough rise on a warm place for 12–18 hours, until it doubles in size.
  5. On a heavily floured surface, fold dough into a ball.
  6. Transfer the ball of dough to a piece of parchment paper. Cover it with the upside down bowl.
  7. Use the parchment paper to transfer the dough into the hot pot and let the bread bake on it.

    Bake the dough:

    450 °F30 minutesEmpty covered pot
    30 minutesBread in covered pot
    15 minutesBread in uncovered pot
  8. Let it rest on a cooling rack.

Serving§

1 loaf.
