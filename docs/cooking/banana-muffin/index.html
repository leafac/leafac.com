Banana Muffin

Ingredients§

Golden flaxseed meal2 tablespoons
Water5 tablespoons
Ripe bananas4
Light brown sugar1/2 cup
Coconut oil1/4 cup
Vanilla extract2 teaspoons
Baking soda2 teaspoons
Salt1/8 teaspoon
Oats1/2 cup
Bleached all-purpose flour1 1/2 cup
Light brown sugar1/4 cup
Bleached all-purpose flour5 tablespoons
Coconut oil4 tablespoons

Baking§

375 °F20 minutesBig muffin
16 minutesSmall muffin

Directions§

  1. On a bowl, mix ingredients for flax egg:

    Golden flaxseed meal2 tablespoons
    Water5 tablespoons
  2. Wait for 5 minutes.

  3. Add the bananas and smash them with a fork until only small chunks remain:

    Ripe bananas4

  4. Mix in the rest of the ingredients for the dough one by one:

    Light brown sugar1/2 cup
    Coconut oil1/4 cup
    Vanilla extract2 teaspoons
    Baking soda2 teaspoons
    Salt1/8 teaspoon
    Oats1/2 cup
    Bleached all-purpose flour1 1/2 cup

  5. On a separate bowl, mix the ingredients for the crumbs:

    Light brown sugar1/4 cup
    Bleached all-purpose flour5 tablespoons
    Coconut oil4 tablespoons
  6. Pour the dough into a muffin pan.
  7. Add the crumbs to the top.

  8. Bake:

    375 °F20 minutesBig muffin
    16 minutesSmall muffin
  9. Let it rest on a cooling rack.

Serving§

8 muffins.
