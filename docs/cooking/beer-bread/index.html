Beer Bread

Ingredients§

Sifted unbleached flour3 cups
Baking powder3 teaspoons
Salt1 teaspoon
Sugar1/4 cup
Beer1 bottle

Baking§

375 °F50 minutes–1 hour

Directions§

  1. On a bowl, mix the dry ingredients:

    Sifted unbleached flour3 cups
    Baking powder3 teaspoons
    Salt1 teaspoon
    Sugar1/4 cup

  2. Mix in the beer:

    Beer1 bottle
  3. Pour dough into a greased loaf or muffin pan.
  4. Brush olive oil on dough.

  5. Bake:

    375 °F50 minutes–1 hour

Serving§

1 beer bread loaf.
