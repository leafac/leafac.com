Bolinho de Chuva

Ingredients§

Golden flaxseed meal2 tablespoons
Water5 tablespoons
Granulated sugar1 cup
Almond milk1 cup
Baking powder1 teaspoon
Bleached all-purpose flour2 1/2 cup

Directions§

  1. On a bowl, mix ingredients for flax egg:

    Golden flaxseed meal2 tablespoons
    Water5 tablespoons
  2. Wait for 5 minutes.

  3. Mix in the ingredients for the base one by one:

    Granulated sugar1 cup
    Almond milk1 cup
    Baking powder1 teaspoon

  4. Mix in the flour:

    Bleached all-purpose flour2 1/2 cup
  5. Deep fry balls of batter shaped with the help of two spoons.

Serving§

15 bolhinhos de chuva.
