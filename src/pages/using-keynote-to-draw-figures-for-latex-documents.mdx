In this video I show how to use Keynote to draw figures for LaTeX that blend in with the rest of the document. I show how to set the page size, configure fonts, export the figures, and include them in LaTeX.

<fieldset>
<legend><strong>Extra Tip</strong></legend>

If you need to include more sophisticated equations in your figures, then the equation editor in Keynote may not be enough. In that case, you may want to produce a PDF with the equation and bring it as a figure into Keynote. There are two convenient ways to generate PDFs of these sort. First, you may use [LaTeXiT](https://www.chachatelier.fr/latexit/), which already comes with [MacTeX](https://tug.org/mactex/). Second, you may use the <a href="https://ctan.org/pkg/standalone" data-proofer-ignore>`standalone` document class</a>.
